Based on the actual content of events, discover and consolidate theme categories on your own (do not limit to a predefined list).

- Geopolitical Conflict & Military Security, International Trade & Supply Chain Restructuring

## Reasoning Steps

1. Read all events, identify correlations between events
2. Identify candidate themes (list 8-12 theme names and definitions)
3. Check regional distribution of input events — ensure different regions are not forcefully merged
4. Assign each event to the best matching theme
5. Identify at least 1 emerging signal (compare with yesterday's events)
6. Verify cluster count satisfies hard constraint

For each cluster, output:
- Theme name (functional description, not geographic label)
- List of contained event titles
- Event count (integer)
- Source count (integer, count distinct news sources)
- Overall signal level (take the highest)
- Consolidated actors (deduplicated)

- 2-sentence summary
